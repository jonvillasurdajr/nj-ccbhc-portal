Whether delivered directly or through a DCO or care coordination agreement, the CCBHC is responsible for ensuring access to all required services under PAMA: crisis services; screening, assessment and diagnosis; person/family-centered treatment planning; outpatient MH and SUD services; outpatient primary care screening and monitoring; comprehensive case management; psychiatric rehabilitation; and peer support and family supports.
Attestation: This CCBHC attests they provide all required services. Yes=currently provides all. In Progress=provides some and has plans for remaining. No=does not provide. If In Progress or No, provide details including implementation timelines in notes.
